Our secret

How did we started? Well, we started with a small group of portuguese friends, some unemployed some not, eager to live in this beautiful city with an improved purpose.


We love our city but ALSO multicultural environments and sharing of travelling experiences, cultures and much more.
In our travels we always felt there was a lack of local information of where to eat, to go, to see as a local. We didn't want to experience as a TOURIST!


That's when we all thought together about opening Local Company For You@ to live as we live in Lisbon.
We were all born in this city with the passion to share with you the best local experience.

Since 2018 sharing a Lisboner life. Join us!
